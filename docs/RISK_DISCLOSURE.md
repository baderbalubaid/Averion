## 2. DCA Strategy Risk

Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA) does not guarantee profit.

Specific risks:
→ Price may continue falling indefinitely
→ Capital can be fully depleted through DCAs
→ Recovery may take weeks, months, or never occur
→ Highly volatile coins may never recover
→ Exchange may delist a coin before recovery

DCA is not suitable for all market conditions.
A bear market lasting years can deplete all capital.
